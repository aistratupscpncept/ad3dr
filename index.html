AD3DR | Research

Autonomous Drone 3D Reconstruction

AD3DR is a concept for 3d applications with L.I.D.A.R technology. For Examples:

  • Terrain reconstruction - For builderds to build in 3d their project in easy way for scanning the terrain when is to built the House.
  • For surveyor - 3D Reconstruction of an place to make simulatio of explosion for optimize the work.

    • And for more type of research

    AD3DR V1

    This is an example of the drone copuled with lidar tecnology. Basically it is a normal drone but main camera is a lidar camera.For Object Detection and Recognition to wiev what the drone sees, we use an application designed for the drone. The lidar camera is for, the three-dimensional reconstruction of you want to see or are tracking with the drone. When the drone is online and the program of the drone is detecting an object would like to scan. The main gross work of scanning is made from the second camera on bottom. With api of Open3D we can detecting our CloudPoint for rendering in 3d. NOTICE: This is an idea of this drone i explain solo the idea on this site nothing is ready.
